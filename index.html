About Igloo

Igloo Refrigerator Parts Inc came from humble beginnings. Our founder, Mr Snowy, started repairing abandoned freezers and refrigerators at the age of 9, in his daddy's barn. It was the only way he could afford to keep his bottles of soda cold.

Today Igloo gives back to the community that helped it grow.
